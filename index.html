Live Vinyl

Created an app that represents music in three different forms: live, vinyl, and digital. The purpose of this app is to allow a user to search any artist that they love, and then show all the current tour dates, albums on Spotify, vinyl releases, and related artists. Users can create a log in and save vinyl and tour dates to their profile.
